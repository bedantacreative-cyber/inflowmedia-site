How It Works

From Zero to Content Machine

01
Strategy Call
We audit your current brand, your ICP, your offer, and your competition. You get a full content strategy on the call — topic pillars, hook angles, platform priority, and a 30-day launch plan.
02
Script + Record
We send you 4–8 scripts per month. You record on your phone or camera — we'll give you a simple setup guide if needed. Send us the raw footage. That's it from you.
03
Edit + Approve
We edit, add captions, music, hooks. You review a Google Drive folder of finished videos. Approve or give one round of feedback. Usually takes less than 10 minutes of your time.

We post on the schedule, track performance, and report weekly. Month over month, your numbers grow and your content gets more dialled in. You focus on your business.
