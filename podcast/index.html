The Process

From Recording
To Viral Clip

01
You Record
Record your episode as you normally would. Send us the raw file — video or audio only. We handle everything from there. No extra work on your end.
02
We Edit + Clip
Our editors go through the full episode, pull the 30 best moments, rewrite hooks, add captions, and format for every platform. Delivered in a shared Google Drive folder within 5 business days.
03
You Approve
We send you a content calendar with every clip scheduled. You review, give feedback, or approve. One round of revisions included.
04
We Distribute
We post everything according to the calendar — across Instagram, YouTube Shorts, and TikTok. Analytics tracked weekly. You see results by the end of month one.
