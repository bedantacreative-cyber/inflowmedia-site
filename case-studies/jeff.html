What We Built

The System

We implemented a strategic content system combining trend monitoring, competitor analysis, and our LIST framework to create authority-driven content that consistently attracted and engaged the right audience.

L
List the Problems
Every piece of content starts by highlighting the exact pain points Jeff's audience faces. Women struggling with fat loss, tired of conflicting advice, frustrated with slow progress. Lead with their reality first — make them feel seen before you say anything else.
I
Answer with Authority
Position Jeff as the solution with expert insights drawn from 18+ years of coaching and 2000+ transformations. Every answer reinforces his authority in the niche and builds trust before any ask is made.
S
Story and Social Proof
Client transformations, real results, relatable analogies woven into every video. Proof that makes the audience think "that could be me." Stories stick where facts slide off. This is what converts views into followers.
T
Takeaway Every Time
End every video with one actionable insight the viewer can use immediately. This builds the habit of coming back — and it's what converts followers into booked calls and paying clients over time.
+
Content OS and Full Distribution
Trend and competitor analysis to identify topics that actually drive growth in Jeff's niche. A strategic posting system with consistent scheduling. Full production, editing, captions, and distribution handled entirely by our team — Jeff just records.
